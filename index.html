Our Story

Welcome to Alina Bakery

Established in 2025, Alina Bakery brings you the finest artisan baked goods made with passion and quality ingredients. Every loaf, pastry, and treat is crafted with care in our home kitchen, bringing warmth and joy to your table.

We believe in the art of traditional baking, using time-honored techniques combined with creative flair. From our signature sourdough breads to delicate pastries and irresistible cookies, each item is baked fresh to order.
